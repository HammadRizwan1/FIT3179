Earthquake Visualization

Earthquake Properties Bubble Chart

The "Earthquake Properties Bubble Chart" is a dynamic and informative visualization that offers a comprehensive view of earthquake characteristics, combining depth, significance, alert level, and magnitude in a single graphical representation. This chart is a powerful tool for understanding and comparing earthquake properties across a range of seismic events.

Earthquake Magnitude Distribution

The "Earthquake Magnitude Distribution" graph provides a visual representation of the distribution of earthquake magnitudes over a specific period. This graph offers valuable insights into the frequency and intensity of seismic activity, which is a fundamental aspect of understanding earthquake patterns.

Average Depth by Location

The "Average Depth by Location" graph displays the average depth of earthquakes by country. It provides insights into how earthquake depths vary across different countries.

Earthquake Frequencies

the "Time Series of Earthquake Frequencies" chart shows how the number of earthquakes has varied over time. It helps in understanding the temporal patterns of earthquake occurrences.
